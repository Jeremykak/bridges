ABOUT US

Bridges College Zana is located 7km off Kampala-Entebbe road, 1/2km Zana market-Nyanama road.Brdges college Zana is a private mixed day and bording school with a touch of professional touch as the founders are long serving teachers. Though the school may appear small but its large number of dedicated teachers gives personal attention to challenge the broad based UNEB curriculum and opportunity to pass highly both 'O' and 'A' Level examinations. We specialize in both Arts and Sciences Subjects. Our cirriculum is comprhensive which includes humanities, science, commercial bussiness, technical (crafts), Music dance and drama, games and sports. Right from the time a student joins our school up to the end he/she is trained beyond justrdinary class work but think like enteprenuers. Their creative minds are tested in different fields while their tallents are explored.

Word from Headteacher

Greetings Beloved! Praise be to God who gives us another year to live in His goodness. Bridges College Zana stands as a beacon of educational excellence, fostering a dynamic learning environment that transcends traditional boundaries. Our School is committed to shaping the future by nurturing young minds and instilling values that extend beyond the classroom. Beyond academics, Mengo Senior School places a premium on character development. We are devoted to moulding well-rounded individuals who contribute meaningfully to society. Our emphasis on moral values, leadership skills, and community service reflects our commitment to producing responsible citizens capable of navigating the complexities of the world. Our impact extends far beyond the the classroom. Our vibrant extracurricular activities provide students with opportunities to explore their talents and passions, be it in Sports, Arts, or various clubs and societies. These activities not only enrich their overall educational experience but also contribute to the development of teamwork, discipline, and a sense of belonging. Our institution recognizes the importance of collaboration with parents, guardians, and the broader community. As a responsible member of the community, Bridges College Zana is committed to exploring our students talents putting them to use . Through these efforts, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of those around us, reinforcing the school's role as a catalyst for positive change. In conclusion, Bridges College Zana is not just an educational institution; it is a nurturing ground for future leaders, innovators, and compassionate individuals. Our unwavering commitment to excellence, both in academics and character development, sets us apart. Together, with the support of our dedicated staff, engaged parents, and the broader community, we continue to shape a brighter future for generations to come. Success is no Accident!
